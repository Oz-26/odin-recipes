Teriyaki Salmon

Description

A flaky, juicy, delicious teriyaki glazed salmon recipe. No lengthy marinating required.

Ingredients

For the Teriyaki Salmon:

  • 2 lbs salmon filet sliced into 2" wide slices

For the Teriyaki Sauce:

  • 3 tbsp teriyaki sauce
  • 3 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp white vinegar
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1⁄3 cup packed light brown sugar
  • 2 large or 3 medium garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tsp freshly grated ginger, or 1⁄,2 tsp ground ginger
  • Sesame seeds and chopped green onion to garnish, optional

Recipe

  1. Grease a large baking sheet (cover with foil and then grease for easier clean-up). Preheat oven to 400 ÂºF.
  2. Combine sauce ingredients and stir until brown sugar is dissolved.
  3. Place individual salmon slices in a large mixing bowl. Pour the sauce over the salmon, cover with plastic wrap and let marinate at least 20 minutes (at room temp or refrigerated).
  4. Transfer salmon to baking sheet (keep the marinade). Bake at 400 for 14-16 min or until salmon is flaky and cooked through.
  5. While salmon is baking, transfer remaining marinade to a small sauce pan and bring to a boil then reduce heat to a simmer and cook, stirring occasionally until slightly thickened (3-4 min) then remove from heat.
  6. Once salmon is out of the oven, brush with teriyaki syrup, then garnish with chopped green onion and sesame seeds as desired.
